CSE 470 HW#1: Sierpinski Gasket Triwl & Color

Author: Jacob Shaeffer       Date: January 24, 2017

Description: This program displays a Sierpinski Gasket after it has been morphed and colored.

Functionality:

  • First each vertex of the original gasket is rotated based on its distance from the origin(The center of the canvas).
  • The equations used to determine rotation are:
    • x' = x * cos(dist * theta) - y * sin(dist * theta)
    • y' = x * sin(dist * theta) + y * cos(dist * theta)
    • Where theta = 45 degrees & dist = sqrt(x^2 + y^2)

  • These new verticies are then colored based on their location in the canvas.
  • The effect is meant to resemble the American flag.

Parameters:

  • The variable NumTimesToSubdivide in gasket2.js controlls the number of times each triangle is sub divided.
  • The verticies, also in gasket2.js, will change the starting position of the triangle before it is morphed.
